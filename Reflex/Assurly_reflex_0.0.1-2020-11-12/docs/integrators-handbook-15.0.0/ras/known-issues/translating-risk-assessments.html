Translating risk assessments

[TRA-1] Translating a risk assessment requires performing an assessment

While translating a risk assessment with the translation resource the risk assessment is assessed again, which means that if the risk assessment is modified by the client these modifications will be taken into account and a new assessment is calculated. Therefore it is recommended that only assessments that are immediately generated by a call to the risk assessment resource and not further modified by a client should be translated by the translation resource.

[TRA-3] Questionnaire in provided data block is not translated

The questionnaire in the provided data block of a risk assessment is not translated by the translation resource.

[TRA-4] A translation may be associated with information loss

When a risk assessment is translated into another language and then back to the original language, all synonyms of assessment factors are replaced by their corresponding main translation.
